Altmarkets 1.3.5.0
=====================

Intro
-----
The Altmarkets(ALTM) blockchain exists in the Duality binary architecture as an asset and in turn for [Dynamic(DYN)](https://github.com/duality-solutions/dynamic) is its real world interface.

Altmarkets is a digital currency that enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world. Altmarkets uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with no central authority (decentralisation): managing transactions and issuing currency (ALTM) are carried out collectively by the Altmarkets network. Altmarkets is the name of open source software which enables the use of the currency ALTM.


Setup
-----
Unpack the files into a directory and run Altmarkets-qt.exe.

Altmarkets is the original Altmarkets client and it builds the backbone of the network.
However, it downloads and stores the entire history of Altmarkets transactions;
depending on the speed of your computer and network connection, the synchronization
process can take anywhere from a few hours to a day or more.
